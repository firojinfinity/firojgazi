About

Dr. Firoj Gazi is an interdisciplinary researcher specializing in IoT, IoUT, and intelligent communication systems. His work focuses on energy-efficient underwater networks, reinforcement learning-based protocols, and AI-driven solutions. He actively develops scalable systems for real-world deployment in smart environments and resource-constrained networks.

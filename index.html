About

Dr. Firoj Gazi is an interdisciplinary researcher specializing in Internet of Things (IoT), Internet of Underwater Things (IoUT), and intelligent communication systems. His research focuses on energy-efficient underwater acoustic networks, reinforcement learning-based MAC protocols, and AI-driven edge systems for real-world deployments in smart environments.

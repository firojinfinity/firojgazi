About

Dr. Firoj Gazi is a researcher specializing in Internet of Things (IoT), Internet of Underwater Things (IoUT), and intelligent communication systems. His work focuses on energy-efficient and reliable protocols for underwater acoustic networks using reinforcement learning and SDN.

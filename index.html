About

Dr. Firoj Gazi is a researcher specializing in Internet of Things (IoT), Internet of Underwater Things (IoUT), and intelligent communication systems. His research focuses on energy-efficient underwater acoustic networks, reinforcement learning-based protocol design, and AI-driven edge systems. He works on real-world deployments in smart cities, healthcare AI, and resource-constrained environments.

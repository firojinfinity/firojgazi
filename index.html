About

Dr. Firoj Gazi is a researcher specializing in the Internet of Things (IoT), Internet of Underwater Things (IoUT), and intelligent communication systems. His work focuses on designing scalable, energy-efficient, and reliable protocols for resource-constrained environments, particularly underwater acoustic networks. He has over 5+ years of research experience and has contributed to high-impact venues including IEEE IoT Journal, IEEE Systems Journal, ACM Transactions, and IEEE GLOBECOM. His research integrates reinforcement learning, software-defined networking (SDN), and real-world IoT deployments.

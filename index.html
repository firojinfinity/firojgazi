About

Dr. Firoj Gazi is an interdisciplinary researcher and academician with expertise spanning Internet of Things (IoT), Internet of Underwater Things (IoUT), and intelligent communication systems. His work focuses on designing energy-efficient, reliable, and scalable solutions for underwater acoustic networks and next-generation IoT ecosystems. His research integrates advanced techniques such as reinforcement learning, edge intelligence, and software-defined networking to address real-world challenges in communication, environmental monitoring, and smart systems. He is actively involved in developing innovative AI-driven solutions with practical deployments in resource-constrained environments.
